Design System · v1

MySafeSpeak

A visual language for anonymous workplace reporting. Every choice here serves one idea: the reporter's identity stays hidden, but their report is taken seriously — visible, tracked, and acted on.

Color

Palette

No red alarm-panel palette — a report already carries enough tension. Ink and Harbor teal carry the weight of trust and discretion; warm Signal and Moss mark status without shouting; Brick is reserved only for the most urgent categories.

Typography

Type

Space Grotesk gives headings a procedural, official edge. Inter carries the reading — reports, chat, help text. JetBrains Mono is reserved for anything that functions like a case-file artifact: tracking tokens, IDs, timestamps, audit logs — data that must read as exact and unforgeable.
