Frequently asked questions

Can my employer find out who filed a report?

No. Your identity is never shown to managers, investigators, or admins — only your company email is used once, to confirm you're an employee.

How do I check my report without logging in?

Use the Track Report page and enter the tracking token you received after submitting.

Can I edit a report after submitting?

You can't edit the original submission, but you can add more evidence or context anytime through the chat with your investigator.

What if my report is marked "fake"?

You can appeal directly from the report's page. Your appeal is reviewed independently.
