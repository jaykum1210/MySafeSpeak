Frequently asked

The questions we hear most from employees before they file their first report.

Can my company find out who I am?

No. Your account is never linked to your name in any view an investigator, manager, or admin can see. Only your company email is used to verify you're an employee during signup.

Do I need an account to check my report?

No — your tracking token alone lets you view status updates without logging in.

What can I attach as evidence?

Photos, videos, audio, documents, PDFs, and screenshots. Everything is encrypted on upload.

What happens if I disagree with the outcome?

Eligible cases can be appealed directly from your report's detail page.
